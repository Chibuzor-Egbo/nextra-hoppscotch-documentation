# Linked Accounts

## Introduction

A linked account is an external bank account that is associated with a user's profile on the platform. This account is used for various financial transactions, including receiving payments, settlements, and managing funds. The functionalities you can take for the linked account include;

**Add linked account:** Users can effortlessly link a new bank account by providing the bank name and account number.

**Remove Linked Account:** Users have the flexibility to unlink bank accounts from their platform profile

**View Linked Account History:** Comprehensive information about linked accounts, including unique ID and transaction history, is accessible for review.
